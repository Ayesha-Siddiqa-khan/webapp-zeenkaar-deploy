# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Style-conscious luxury fashion and beauty shoppers browsing curated edits and wanting confident purchase decisions. Secondary audiences include gift buyers and repeat customers tracking new drops.

## Product Purpose
Curate premium fashion and beauty essentials with fast discovery, clear product storytelling, and a smooth path to checkout. Support post-purchase confidence through order tracking in the dashboard.

## Brand Personality
Elevated, warm, modern.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS dashboards, discount-heavy or flashy retail UIs, neon streetwear aesthetics.

## Design Principles
1. Curated clarity over endless choice.
2. Confidence through detail and materials.
3. Frictionless purchase flow from browse to checkout.
4. Quiet luxury, not loud luxury.
5. Post-purchase reassurance with transparent tracking.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA contrast targets, reduced motion respected, clear focus states and form labels.
